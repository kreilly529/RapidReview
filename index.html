Bonus Eligibility Policy
  • Active payrolled employee — Must be an active payrolled employee during the entire semi-annual period (180 day period) to be eligible, with exception of new hires and employees on approved leave of absence (see below).
  • New hires — New hires will obtain eligibility in the next full quarter following their hire. Bonus is prorated to half the semi-annual amount earned if eligibility is obtained halfway through the semi-annual period. Manager will review quantitative measures with new hire during onboarding.
  • Leave of absence — Employees on leave of absence in excess of 5 days during a quarter of the semi-annual period will not be eligible for full payment. The bonus will be prorated to half the semi-annual amount for a full quarter worked within the semi-annual period.
  • Active at time of distribution — Must be an active payrolled employee at the time of distribution to be eligible for payment. Should an employee leave PrintMail prior to the distribution of payment, for any reason (voluntary or involuntary), no payment will be made by PrintMail.
  • Good standing — Employee must be in good standing with the company. Employees who are subject to active disciplinary action, on a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP), or have documented performance issues with their core job functions are not eligible for bonus payment.
This employee does not meet one or more eligibility requirements. The bonus amount will be $0. The remainder of the review will still be completed for documentation and development purposes.
